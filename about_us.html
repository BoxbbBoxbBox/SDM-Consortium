About Us

The Supported Decision-Making Consortium is made of dozens of individuals from many different backgrounds.

  • Self-advocates: People with disabilities who support the use of SDM.
  • Service coordinators: Professionals who help people access resources within their community.
  • Family members: People with loved ones who use or want to use SDM.
  • Public administrators: Professional guardians in the state of Missouri who promote SDM.
  • Attorneys Lawyers who include SDM in their practice.

Leadership

The Supported Decision-Making Consortium is led by Missouri's DD Network and its three partners: UMKC-IHD, the DD Council, and Mo P&A
